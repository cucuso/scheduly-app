How to use

You can change months with left and right arrow keys. When you need to schedule an appointment just double click on the day and add the information. you can edit the appointment by double clicking it as well. Input the phone number without any brackets or any dashes just the seven digit number you wish to notify. You can delete an appointment by selecting it and pressing delete key.

Security

Scheduly is end to end encrypted. All payments processed with Scheduly are done through Stripe so we never have access to any credit card information making us PCI compliance. Stripe has been audited by a PCI-certified auditor and is certified to PCI Service Provider Level 1. This is the most stringent level of certification available in the payments industry. To accomplish this, we make use of best-in-class security tools and practices to maintain a high level of security at Stripe.
