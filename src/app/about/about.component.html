What is Scheduly

Scheduly provides free mobile text alerts for fifteen days and then after that there is small fee (We have to pay server costs and text messaging rates), which allows business owners to maintain a calendar with all the scheduled appointments, and Scheduly automatically sends them a SMS message.

How to setup text alerts

You can change months with left and right arrow keys. When you need to schedule an appointment just double click on the day and add the information. you can edit the appointment by double clicking it as well. Input the phone number without any brackets or any dashes just the seven digit number you wish to notify. You can delete an appointment by selecting it and pressing delete key.

Security

Scheduly is end to end encrypted. All payments processed with Scheduly are done through Stripe so we never have access to any credit card information making us PCI compliance. Stripe has been audited by a PCI-certified auditor and is certified to PCI Service Provider Level 1. This is the most stringent level of certification available in the payments industry. To accomplish this, we make use of best-in-class security tools and practices to maintain a high level of security at Stripe.

Benefits of using a SMS reminder service

1.Reduce No-Shows and Waiting Lists

No shows are a costly problem in any business. Letting a time slot go to waste because a customer forgot to show up, or cancel can cost businesses thousands. With this product you will be proactive in solving this issue and it has been proven sending a notification a day prior will reduct no shows by an immense number.

2.Increase Customer Satisfaction

Using this sofware improves overall customer satisfaction. Customers feel good about being reminded about upcoming via text message, a method that is not intrusive, and they can respond at a time that is most convenient for them.
